Green Choice Mara is a community based organization located in Narok town. We majorly target schools and institution is order to educate children to plant trees and conserve the environment. We aim to ensure that we leave every place we visit greener and the people more educated about climate change adaptation and mitigation. We also focus on empwering wonemand even the girl child by providing free sanitary towels to the girls. We also insist on the importance of copletion of education to ensure that every child, both boys and girls are able to reach their maximum potential and molud a future for themselves.

Vision

To come up with innovations that are geared towards climate change adaptation and mitigation.

Mission

To regreen and restore environment and improve the relationship between nature and humans.

Main Objective

To empower communities especially women on environmental conservation and sustainable developement.

Specific Objectives

1.Educating and involving the local communuty in conservation activities such as tree planting and its significance.
2.Empowering girl child through mentorship, education and skill developement.
3.Educating the youth and local community in income generating activities such as how to raise seedbeds and generating income from the sale of tree seedlings.
4.Empowering and intergrating women indeginous knowledge on conservation in climate change adaptation.
5.Educating and raising awarenenss on conservation and on how humans and wildlife can peacefully co-exist in the mara ecosystem.
